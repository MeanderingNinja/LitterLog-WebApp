Project Story: How this cat project came to be.

In late 2019, our family cat Atticus was diagnosed with Feline lower urinary tract disease (FLUTD). He had the symptoms of trouble urinating and had been seen squatting in his litterbox longer than usual.

Medications were issued to Atticus and we were advised to monitor his bathroom behavior to see if his straining to urinate would alleviate. It was at that time when the idea of making an automatic cat bathroom Monitoring System came into being.

The bathroom monitoring system can be used not only for when a cat is diagnosed with FLUTD, but also for preventative health monitoring.
